About Us


Lil’Touch or Lily Touch is Indonesia's trusted and most complete online shopping destination offering luxury beauty products serving women across Indonesia. As a professional manufacturing leader in the cosmetic online shopping, our services include bespoke products, private label, personalization. Lil’Touch sell beauty products in a highly professional way engaging businesses for overseas retailers, small brands and enterprises.


Quality and authenticity of beauty product is akin to the importance of taking care of your own skin, a necessity to all women and Lil’Touch takes it very seriously. Usage of non-original or fraudulent beauty products could lead to permanent skin damage. This has prompted Lil’Touch to collaborate directly with authorized national distributors and brand owners in all over the world to ensure that every product you purchase from Lil’Touch are original and authentic, with the certification from Badan Pengawas Obat dan Makanan (‘BPOM’). Quality assurance of product storage is performed directly by Lil’Touch's own operations management with compliance to international standards and storage guideline by the brand owners.
